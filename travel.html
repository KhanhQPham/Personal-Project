Mount Fuji

At Mount Fuji, you can ski, snowman, take a walk on the snowy roads and spoil for shooting beautiful photos. On Mount Fuji, many popular ski resorts, luxurious resorts and even traditional Japanese houses await you to stay.

Tokyko Tower

Every year, Tokyo Tower attracts thousands of tourists. You know, this is one of the few prime locations, allowing you to take a panoramic view of beautiful Tokyo when the city lights up. Feeling overwhelmed, surprised by a colorful picture of flickering lights, reflecting the prosperity and modernity.

Monkey Park Jigoku Dani

Have you ever seen the lovely monkeys, playing mischievously in the freezing snow? Come to Jigoku Dani Monkey Park to experience incomparable things in turn. Hot spring baths are too familiar but hot spring baths with monkeys make sure you haven't tried them. Believe me, this will be one of the most memorable bathing times in my life.

Himeji Castle

Himeji Castle is an extremely interesting Japanese tourist destination that you should definitely visit if possible to Himeji City in Hyogo Prefecture. The castle is made of over 36 tons of wood and white plaster like cranes flying in the vast sky.

French Riviera

The Riviera coast is also known as Côte d'Azur, which is an ideal destination for those who have been addicted to the beauty of the Mediterranean sea. Along this famous coastline are many famous tourist cities of France. Typical is Nice, Cannes or the Principality of Monaco.

Biarritz

A resort town on the Atlantic coast has long beaches. It will be an ideal destination for those who love surfing.

Namsan

Leading the list of the most interesting destinations in Korea, NamSan Tower attracts a large number of tourists visiting every day. Impressed by the visitors visiting the tower is an impressive love space. In addition to the "Love Tiles" wall, the most floating feature here is the "Lover's Lock". The lovers hang locks at the terrace located right at the entrance of the tower with a piece of message paper as a symbol of their love.
